History

Born from a passion for adventure and a love for the wild, White Water Rafting Company began in 2005 with a single raft and a dream. Over the years, we've guided more than 10,000 thrill-seekers down the most breathtaking rivers in the West. Our founders, once river guides themselves, believed that every rapid tells a story and every journey forges new friendships. Today, our team of certified guides is dedicated to making every trip safe, memorable, and absolutely exhilarating.

Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned paddler, we promise an experience filled with laughter, teamwork, and the pure joy of conquering the rapids together. Join us and become part of our ever-growing river family!
